Looking ahead

Beyond completing the diagram, there are a number of longer-term ambitions for this project.

A collection of building society passbooks is currently being assembled, with members' passbooks from as many existing societies as possible. These will eventually be displayed here, both as a gallery in their own right and as part of each society's individual page.

Archive visits are also planned — many building society records, minute books, and historical documents are held in county record offices and local studies libraries around the country that have never been digitised. These visits would allow the history sections to go significantly deeper than what is currently possible through newspaper archives and published records alone.

If you would like to support the project — whether to help cover research costs, archive travel, or the British Newspaper Archive subscription that makes much of this possible — a Ko-fi page will be available in due course.

Finally, a companion project is in the works: a catalogue and display of a personal collection of bank cards from the UK and beyond, to be linked from this site when ready.
