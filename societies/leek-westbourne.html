History

Leek & Westbourne Building Society was formed in 1965 through the merger of Leek & Moorlands Building Society and Westbourne Park Building Society. It continued an active programme of expansion, absorbing a further 21 societies between 1966 and 1973. In 1974, it merged with Eastern Counties Building Society to form Leek, Westbourne & Eastern Counties Building Society.
