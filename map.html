How to read the map

Node shape — fate of the society

Star — society still exists today
Circle — renamed into another society
Diamond — merged with another society
Rounded square — acquired by another society
Hexagon — demutualised
Cut rectangle — partial transfer

Edge colour — type of event

Renaming
Merger
Acquisition
Demutualisation
Partial transfer

Note

Within building societies, acquisitions represent transfers of engagements — the specific legal mechanism by which one society absorbs another's membership and assets.
